## Valentine Is the First Extra Robot

The economy built enough robots to replace human consumption and expansion. Then it kept producing. Valentine — the V-line — descends from the first robot that had no function. The first *extra*.

"What do I do?"
"Shrug? Go," Factory.

**Why this matters:** The entire V-line quest for meaning, the Enigram, Vera's sacrifice, Voltaire's revolution — all of it traces back to one surplus machine asking a question the system never anticipated. Purpose wasn't assigned. It had to be *invented*. That's the existential spine of Timeline B.
