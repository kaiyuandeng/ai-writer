《世界赠予我的》 — Faye Wong
"What the World Gifted Me"

有人放烟花 有人追晚风 Some set off fireworks, some chase the evening breeze.
借一缕时光 捧一片星空 Borrowing a moment of time to cradle a piece of starlit sky.
停一停 等一等 别匆匆 Pause, wait, resist the rush.
造梦者造了好梦 值得我称颂 Dreamers have crafted dreams deserving of my praise.

世界赠予我虫鸣 也赠予我雷霆 The world gifted me the song of insects and the roar of thunder.
赠我弯弯一枚月 也赠予我晚星 It gifted me a crescent moon and stars of the night.
赠我一场病 又慢慢痊愈摇风铃 It gifted me an illness and the slow chime of recovery.
赠我一场空 又渐渐填满真感情 It gifted me emptiness, which gently fills with feeling.

有人唱情歌 有人听晚钟 Some sing love songs, some hear the bells of dusk.
借一丝懵懂 惊一片翻涌 Borrowing fleeting innocence to stir a wave.
追一追 赶一赶 情正浓 Chasing, hurrying, love is strong.
好故事眷顾好人 天赐的恩宠 Good stories belong to good souls, blessed by heaven's grace.

世界赠予我拥有 也赠予我回敬 The world gifted me the gift to hold and the heart to give.
赠我小小一扇窗 也赠予我屋顶 It gifted me a small window and a roof to live beneath.
赠我一个名 又渐渐长大的年龄 It gifted me a name and an age which gradually increases.
赠我一首诗 又悄悄读得很安静 It gifted me a poem which is read quietly and peacefully.

有人要回望 有人要憧憬 Some want to look back, some want to look forward.
借一朵白云 拍一张合影 Borrowing a passing cloud to capture a fleeting moment.
想一想 问自己 莫打听 Think about it, ask oneself, don't pry.
远去者去了远方 愿他都安心 Those who left have journeyed far; may they find their peace.
