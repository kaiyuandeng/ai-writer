The people:

In the Bruce arc, the people are post-modern. They live in hedonistic luxury without much purpose. It’s all about status. (Think San Francisco) 

In the Hyfe arc, the people are earnest and eager. Their purpose is clear, survive. Humans are no longer apex and this concentrates their lives. (Think India/Varinasi)
