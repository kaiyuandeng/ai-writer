“I know Tokonomi (トコノミ) may have wronged you. I am here to tell you: my master is dead. My master was a good man with a troubled past and though he had been good with me in his old age, in his youth he was cruel to many people. If you wish to take revenge upon him but find it too late, you may challenge me, his last pupil, to a duel of your choice.”

“Im sorry Master, I have failed you,” Anais sobbed into the side of her master. Master Oolong Dao-Su coughed out blood.
“Why are you sorry?”
“I failed. War is coming. What should I do?” she cried.
“You fight,” he said, looking at her, “you fight with all your heart.”
She nodded.
“Atha,” he said.
“Atha,” she replied. She held him past his last breath.
