The Buddha’s First Words After Enlightenment
poem by Kai from SN Goenka’s translation of the Pali Canon


In this flow of the world
I have taken birth countless lives

From birth, I run toward death, incessantly
having achieved nothing

I search for the Creator
the Creator of this house

What house?
That house readied for me

upon every birth,
again and again, full of misery

Oh! Builder of the House,
You are seen

I have seen you.
You shall build no more houses for me.

Destroyed all the materials, I have,
eradicated from my mind

all the etched scars that incarnate,
all cravings, rooted out

the cycle broken
I am free
